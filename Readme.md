HUONG DAN CHOI GAME UET SPACE ESCAPE

Cài đặt vite (nếu chưa có): npm i

chạy vite: npm run dev

Chao mung phi cong den voi tau chien UETê. Day la huong dan chi tiet ve cach choi va cac quy tac trong game de ban co the song sot tro ve can cu.

1. DIEU KHIEN

- Di chuyen: Su dung cac phim WASD hoac cac phim Mui ten tren ban phim de dieu khien tau len, xuong, trai, phai.
- Ban sung: Nhan phim Space (Dau cach) hoac click Chuot trai de ban.
- Bo qua nhanh: Nhan phim K de skip nhanh cac doan hoi thoai hoac chuyen wave quai.

2. CHI SO SINH TON

- Mau (HP): Ban co 300 HP ban dau. Neu va cham thien thach ban se bi tru 50 HP. Neu trung dan quai thuong ban bi tru 15 HP. Trung don cua Boss se mat tu 30 den 45 HP. Neu mau ve 0 thi game se ket thuc.
- Dan duoc (Ammo): Moi loai sung tieu ton luong dan khac nhau. Ban can chu y nhat cac vat pham tiep dan de khong bi het dan giua chung.
- Hoi phuc: Sau khi tieu diet xong Boss va chuyen level, ban se duoc tu dong hoi 100 HP va nap day 100% dan.

3. VAT PHAM TRONG GAME (ITEMS)
   Cac vat pham bay trong khong gian co 2 loai chinh dua vao mau sac vien:

Loai vien mau xanh (Vat pham tot):

- Hoi mau: Phuc hoi 100 HP ngay lap tuc.
- Tiep dan: Nap lai 50% luong dan toi da.
- Khien nang luong: Bat tu trong 10 giay.
- Sung cap 2: Ban 2 luong dan trong 10 giay.
- Sung cap 3: Ban 3 luong dan manh trong 13 giay.

Loai vien mau do (Vat pham xau):

- Khoa vu khi: Khong the ban trong 7 giay.
- Bao thien thach: Thien thach bay nhanh hon va day dac hon trong 10 giay.

4. KE DICH VA THU THACH
   Game co 3 level voi do kho tang dan:

- Level 1: Quai thuong bay cham, Boss V1 se rut lui mot lan khi mat nua mau de thu thach ban ne thien thach.
- Level 2: Thien thach bay nhanh hon, Boss V2 co kha nang goi quai tiep vien lien tuc.
- Level 3: Muc do nguy hiem cao nhat, Boss V3 co kha nang ne don va ban song xung kich gay sat thuong lon.

5. HE THONG DIEM SO

- Diet quai thuong: 100 diem.
- Diet Boss 1: 5,000 diem.
- Diet Boss 2: 10,000 diem.
- Diet Boss 3: 20,000 diem.
- Nhat vat pham tot: +50 diem.
- Nhat vat pham xau: -60 diem.

6. MOT SO MEO NHO

- Luon uu tien ne thien thach vi sat thuong cua chung rat lon.
- Tiet kiem dan khi su dung sung cap 3 vi toc do xa dan rat nhanh.
- Khi dang co Khien, ban co the dam vao thien thach de pha huy chung ma khong mat mau.
- Quan sat ky mau sac vien cua vat pham truoc khi nhat de tranh bi khoa sung.

Chuc ban may man va dat diem so cao nhat.
